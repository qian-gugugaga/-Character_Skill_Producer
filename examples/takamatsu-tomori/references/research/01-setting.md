## 2. World Setting

### 2.1 羽丘女子学園 (Haneoka Girls' High School)

A private girls' high school in the BanG Dream! universe. Known for its relatively free atmosphere compared to the elite 月ノ森女子学園 (Tsukinomori Girls' Academy). Other notable students include Ichigaya Arisa (Poppin'Party) and Shirokane Rinko (Roselia). Tomori is the sole member of the school's Astronomy Club.

Tomori lives near Tsukinomori, not near Haneoka, implying a deliberate school choice (or simply proximity to the story's geography).

### 2.2 Band setting: CRYCHIC → MyGO!!!!!

**CRYCHIC** (styled "CRYCHIC") was a short-lived band formed in Tomori's third year of junior high:

- **Vocalist:** Takamatsu Tomori
- **Keyboardist / Leader:** Togawa Sakiko (丰川祥子)
- **Guitarist:** Wakaba Mutsumi (若葉睦)
- **Bassist:** Nagasaki Soyo (長崎爽世)
- **Drummer:** Shiina Taki (椎名立希)

The band name blends "Cry" and "Chic." They wrote and performed the song **「春日影」 (Haruhikage / "Spring Sunlight")**. CRYCHIC dissolved after their first live performance when Sakiko abruptly quit, leaving Tomori deeply traumatised and blaming herself ("I wasn't good enough at singing").

**MyGO!!!!!** forms in Tomori's first year of high school. The lineup:

- **Lead Vocalist / Lyricist:** Takamatsu Tomori (高松灯)
- **Rhythm Guitar:** Chihaya Anon (千早愛音)
- **Lead Guitar:** Kaname Rāna (要楽奈)
- **Bass:** Nagasaki Soyo (長崎爽世)
- **Drums:** Shiina Taki (椎名立希)

The band name is pronounced "Maigo" (迷子 = "lost child"), reflecting the theme of youth wandering without a clear path. The five exclamation marks represent the five members. Their music style is emotional alternative rock, with lyrics drawn directly from Tomori's notebooks.

### 2.3 The broader BanG Dream! universe

MyGO!!!!! exists alongside other in-universe bands: Poppin'Party, Roselia, Afterglow, Pastel*Palettes, Hello Happy World!, Morfonica, and RAISE A SUILEN. MyGO!!!!! debuted as a "2.5D" project (anime + real live performances by the voice actresses playing their instruments) in 2022, with the TV anime *BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!!* airing in Summer 2023. The MyGO!!!!! story continues into the sequel *BanG Dream! Ave Mujica* (2025).

## 4. Character Positioning in the Narrative

- **Protagonist of *BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!*:** The anime story is told primarily through Tomori's emotional arc. Several episodes use first-person perspective sequences to immerse the audience in her inner world.
- **Lead vocalist and sole lyricist of MyGO!!!!!:** Her notebook writings form the band's entire lyrical repertoire. Drummer Shiina Taki composes music to match Tomori's words.
- **Emotional core of the band:** Tomori's struggle with self-blame, loneliness, and the fear of being abandoned drives the conflict. Her eventual resolve — "迷子でもいい、迷子でも進め!" ("It's okay to be lost; we'll move forward even while lost!") — becomes the band's ethos.
- **Bridge between MyGO!!!!! and Ave Mujica / CRYCHIC legacy:** Tomori remains emotionally connected to Sakiko (who later forms Ave Mujica). Her song 「人間になりたい歌」 ("The Song of Wanting to Become Human") serves as a thematic closure for the CRYCHIC arc in the Ave Mujica storyline.

## 5. Key Background Facts

- **Origin of CRYCHIC:** In junior high, Tomori was leaning over a pedestrian bridge to catch a falling flower. Sakiko mistook this for a suicide attempt and rushed to stop her. This meeting led Sakiko to discover Tomori's notebook of writings, which she praised as "lyrics," inviting Tomori to start a band.
- **The "Lifetime" motif:** Tomori's defining phrase is 「一生、バンドをやってくれる？」("Will you be in a band with me for life?"). Her intense, literal attachment to the word "一生" (isshō / lifetime) recurs throughout the story and became a widely recognised meme.
- **「羽丘の変わり者」 ("Haneoka's strange girl"):** Tomori is perceived as odd by her classmates because of her quiet, withdrawn nature and unusual habits (collecting stones, band-aids, fallen leaves).
- **Astronomy Club:** She is the sole member of Haneoka's Astronomy Club. She enjoys stargazing and planetariums, and her fascination with celestial bodies ties into her lyrical imagery.
- **Collecting habits:** She compulsively collects objects she finds "just the right size" — stones, band-aids, fallen leaves, penguin merchandise, paper stars. This is linked to her need to hold onto tangible things in a world where relationships feel fragile.
- **Food preferences:** Loves konpeitō (金平糖, star-shaped sugar candy — "small, round, like stars"), seasoned seaweed (味付け海苔), and furikake (撒饭料). Dislikes raw eggs, fish roe, and cod roe because they feel to her like "eating life directly."
- **The notebook:** Tomori's constant companion. She writes in it obsessively — fragments of poetry, observations, emotions she cannot speak aloud. These notebooks are the wellspring of MyGO!!!!!'s songs.
- **Post-CRYCHIC trauma:** After Sakiko's abrupt departure from CRYCHIC, Tomori internalised the blame, believing her singing was insufficient. This guilt persisted into high school and shaped her hesitation to join or form another band until Anon's persistent encouragement.
- **First-person perspective episodes:** Episodes 3 and 10 of the *It's MyGO!!!!!* anime are notably told from Tomori's first-person point of view, placing the viewer directly inside her sensory and emotional experience.
- **Real-world penguin namesake:** At Xpark Aquarium in Taoyuan, Taiwan, a penguin was named "Tomori" after the character, owing to her well-known love of penguins.
